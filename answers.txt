Q1: Why might be consider using git?

Answer: Git is immensely useful for organization and communication between 
machines as well as people. There are also many options that help reduce 
errors or mistakes with the use of branches. I used git during my internship 
last summer, and saw how valuable the real life applications are. 

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give and Example and how 
it might help

Answer: Data science could be and is applied to virtually every field. A 
field in which it could continue to be applied is the healthcare field. 
Having more accurate data about patients should allow for faster 
improvement of care.
